### Key design decisions (based on actual Flyte 2.0 API)

The Flyte 2.0 API (`flyte` v2.2.3) differs slightly from the guide's pseudocode:

- Two `TaskEnvironment` instances are created: `env` (bare) and `env_with_secret` (carries `API_SECRET`), matching the spec's intent of declaring the secret alongside the environment.
