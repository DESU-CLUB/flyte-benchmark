## serve.py — entry point

Imports `app_env` from `app.py` and starts the server:

1. **Primary path** — calls `flyte.serve(app_env, mode="local")`, letting Flyte drive uvicorn via `FastAPIAppEnvironment._fastapi_app_server`.
2. **Fallback** — if Flyte's local-serve infra raises (no cluster config), falls back to a direct `uvicorn.Server` on port 8000.
